Who am I

Hi there! I’m a UX designer with 7 years of experience creating user-friendly designs that make life easier (and a little more fun). I love diving into what people need and turning that into designs that feel natural and intuitive. Whether it’s building wireframes, refining prototypes, or creating design systems, I’m all about solving problems and making things work better for real people. Collaboration is one of my favorite parts of the process—I enjoy teaming up with others to bring ideas to life.

Skills: UX/UI design, UX research, Prototyping, Accessible design, Visual design, Front-end development, Photography


Tools: Figma, Sketch, Adobe suite, InVision, Visual Studio Code, Principle
