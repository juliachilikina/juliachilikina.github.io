Healthier Kids

foundation

Healthier Kids Foundation is a family forward health agency that gives children and those who love them the education and cutting edge tools they rightfully deserve to live a healthy life. The organization’s website needs to be redesigned to better connect with its audience. The purpose of this redesign project is to simplyfy user experience, build more intuitive information architecture, develope visual consistency, and as a result make it easier for the users to reach their goals on the site.
